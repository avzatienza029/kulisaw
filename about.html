OUR STORY
In April 2020, the Founder thought of opening a business because her husband has been working with multiple projects and he needs people to work for him. Since outsourcing and working-from-home have become common practices these days and many Filipinos lose their jobs, why not start with helping them?

The Founder and her High School friend, who is now the Co-Owner of the company decided to team up to open an Outsourcing company based in the Philippines. Why the Philippines?
The Founder is a Natural-born Filipino who studied in different countries and is currently living in Belgium.
English is the country’s second language.
The Philippines is one of the most Western countries in Asia.
Not everyone is aware of this, but most Filipinos are smart, dedicated, passionate, and hardworking especially when being recognized and appreciated. No wonder why many Filipinos employed abroad are successful in their chosen fields.

As Dr. Jose P. Rizal, our National Hero said, “Ang hindi marunong lumingon sa pinanggalingan, ay hindi makakarating sa paroroonan”. In English, “He who does not know how to look back from where he came from, will never get to his destination”. The Founder believes that success can be achieved by taking credit from where someone came from. Having “gratitude” is considered as one of the keys to victory.

In February 2022, after months of thinking what the company name should be, completing and submitting legal documents, our company was registered and named, KULISAW I.T. Services.

Why KULISAW?
Kulisaw (a Cebuano word) is a group of fish that swims in the same direction. Us, as a company, we are a group of people, with different educational and professional backgrounds who will naturally work (swim) together as a team, in a certain degree of order and cooperation.

Our Mission

To be renowned in Belgium as a company with excellence and competence in the field of Information Technology. We aim to engage a team of experts and empower them to deliver exceptional services, guidance, and training to our clients.

Our Vision

Kulisaw is an I.T. outsourcing company based in the Philippines that provides a high-quality result and offers a wide array of services for our clients in Belgium.

Our Services

Our Team is composed of specialists that will cater services related to Customer Service, Mobile Application Development, Website Testing, Statistical Analysis, Data Management, Data Analytics, Feasibility Study, Microfinance, Market Research and even teaching English as a Secondary Language to your children.
